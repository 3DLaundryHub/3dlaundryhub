Questions, answered

How does the per-load pricing work?

One load is up to 7 kg of regular clothing, or up to 5 kg of thick and heavy items (linens, towels, jeans). We weigh with a digital scale at pickup, in front of you, so the final price is transparent.

When do I pay?

After weighing and verification. Pay by cash or GCash — whichever is easier for you.

How fast is Rush service?

As fast as 5 hours from pickup, at 1.5× the regular price. Book an early slot (10AM–1PM pickup) to get same-day delivery.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes — up to 24 hours before your pickup, right on this site. Scroll to Track my booking, enter your booking code and contact number, and use the Reschedule or Cancel buttons. Inside 24 hours, call us at 0917 326 0472.

How do I know my final amount?

After we weigh your laundry at pickup, the confirmed amount appears in Track my booking — look for the green “Final Amount”. Until then you'll see your booking estimate.

What are your hours?

We pick up and deliver every day, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, in one-hour slots you choose at booking.
